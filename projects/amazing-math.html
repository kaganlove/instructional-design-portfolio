About the Project

A-MAZE-ing Math is a gamified, interactive maze challenge developed to increase math fluency and student engagement. By scaffoldding algebraic and geometric problem-solving in a maze environment, it shifts abstract mathematical rules into concrete, action-oriented game dynamics.

Technical Skills Demonstrated

  • Advanced Variables & Trigger Logic: Mastered complex score tracking, countdown timers, and player lives variables in Articulate Storyline.
  • Scaffolded Curriculum Sequencing: Implemented pedagogical design patterns to step users through progressive levels of mathematical complexity.
  • Gamification Mechanics: Integrated player feedback loops, status overlays, and level-completion gating.
  • Custom UI Design: Created clean, intuitive layouts tailored specifically for student engagement.

Why This Matters

Proves competency in building complex visual simulators, gamified feedback loops, and advanced custom-trigger logical variables within authoring tools. It demonstrates the ability to translate technical curriculum into engaging digital products.
