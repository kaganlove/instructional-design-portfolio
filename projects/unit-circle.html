About the Project

Unit Circle Angle Measures is a technical, fully responsive learning asset mapping degree-to-radian measurements and coordinates inside the mathematical Unit Circle. Developed in Articulate Rise, it provides a device-agnostic, interactive study pathway that breaks down complex trigonometric relations into logical visual components.

Technical Skills Demonstrated

  • Rise Responsive Architecture: Utilized standard block grids to compile responsive, high-fidelity media across desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers.
  • Trigonometric Concept Mapping: Built sequential math onboarding logic for coordinate evaluation.
  • Interactive Knowledge Checks: Integrated custom sorting activities and diagnostics.
  • Technical Definitions Chunking: Maintained clean typography and structural separation for coordinate mappings.

Why This Matters

Highlights STEM subject matter translation, capacity to build device-agnostic Rise modules, and precise technical instruction formatting. Proves your capability to design responsive training for technical audiences.
