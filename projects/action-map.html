About the Project

The Action Map Module is an interactive mapping exercise structured to train designers on Cathy Moore's Action Mapping methodology. By focusing training design on real-world business activities rather than abstract information dumping, it equips designers to align their curriculum directly with performance gaps and business metrics.

Technical Skills Demonstrated

  • Cathy Moore's Action Mapping: Applied performance-centric training structures.
  • Interactive Branching Decision Maps: Programmed complex navigation pathways based on user choices.
  • User State Evaluation: Calculated results dynamically to provide personalized diagnostic feedback.
  • Sleek Interface Layouts: Designed high-contrast interface elements for a professional training aesthetic.

Why This Matters

Demonstrates the capability to convert complex instructional theories and consulting frameworks into practical, hands-on designer training tools. Proves you understand how to design training that drives performance.
