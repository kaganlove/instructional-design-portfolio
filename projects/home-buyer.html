About the Project

The Home Buyer Roadmap is an immersive, 3D interactive simulation designed to train first-time home buyers. Leveraging Articulate Storyline's spatial environment capabilities, the module allows learners to maneuver dynamically throughout a virtual room. Users inspect physical layouts and click on highlighted areas to identify positive and negative buying features (such as signs of structural wear versus modern upgrades), helping them feel fully informed and confident during actual property walkthroughs.

Technical Skills Demonstrated

  • 3D Spatial Navigation: Implemented custom 3D room movement within Storyline, allowing the user to navigate the physical environment.
  • Interactive Hotspot Audits: Programmed click triggers on physical elements in the room to evaluate property indicators.
  • State-Based Feedback Variables: Tracked student assessments of "good" versus "bad" features in real-time, providing targeted guided feedback.
  • Reducing Cognitive Load: Structured user-centric navigation overlays and tutorial prompts to ease the complexity of spatial interaction.
