About the Project

The ADDIE Method Walkthrough is a structured, self-paced walkthrough illustrating the core phases of the ADDIE design workflow: Analyze, Design, Develop, Implement, and Evaluate. It guides new instructional designers and stakeholders through the systematic steps required to create high-impact, scalable educational modules.

Technical Skills Demonstrated

  • ADDIE Framework Structuring: Deep understanding of curriculum mapping, target metrics, and instructional delivery modes.
  • Content Chunking: Translated complex process documentation into logical, bite-sized learning nodes.
  • Accessible Slide Layer Management: Constructed slides using optimized layered structures for screen readers and tab indexing.
  • UI Vector Styling: Created original instructional graphics and diagrams to visualize process gates.

Why This Matters

Showcases your standard pedagogical design principles, clear visual hierarchies, and structured conceptual training workflows. It proves you can onboard teams into standardized methodologies efficiently.
